Date 29-Oct-2013

Ticker : NTPC’s tax free bonds

 The Central Board of Direct Taxes (the “CBDT”), by notification no. 61/2013 F.No.178/37/2013-(ITA.1) dated August 8, 2013, has allowed NTPC Limited (the “Company”) to issue tax-free bonds aggregating to Rs. 1,750 crore (the “Bonds”) during financial year 2013-14. As per the notification, the salient features of the Bonds include the following:  

·         Interest income on the Bonds will be tax-free in the hands of eligible investors throughout the tenure of the Bonds.

·         The Bonds may be issued in three tenures: 10, 15 or 20 years.

·         Interest rates offered to Retail Individual Investors (“RIIs”), which include resident/non-resident individuals and Hindu Undivided Families (“HUFs”), will be 0.25% higher than interest rates offered to eligible investors in other categories.

·         The final interest rates offered on the Bonds will be disclosed in the Prospectus filed by the Company with Registrar of Companies.

·         Maximum application size for Retail Individual Investors is Rs. 10,00,000.
